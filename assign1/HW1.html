IC's 125th Birthday!

IC is turning 125! That means it’s time to celebrate a century-and-a-quarter of incredible connections, people, traditions, and impact. We’ve all got our best Ithaca College memories, and now’s the perfect time to come together to share our stories and have a blast. It all kicks off with the Weekend on South Hill, a huge campus celebration for students, alumni, faculty, staff, families, and neighbors. Plus, we'll be inaugurating President Shirley M. Collado that Saturday. Come join the fun November 3-5, 2017.
