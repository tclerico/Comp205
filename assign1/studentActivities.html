Electroacustic Music

Ithaca college's music program offers over 350 concerts each year. Every concert is open free to the public and is put on by students in the music department. On Friday, September 15, there will be an open recital in the Nabenhauer Recital Room for the Electroacustic Music class.

Handwerker Gallery

Ithaca Colleges Handwerker gallery offers students a look into special art exhibits from a wide range of artists. The current gallery is Leslie Brack's Memorium, and will be available for free until October 18. Stop by and look at all the art!

Family Weekend

IC's annual fall family weekedend will be bigger than ever this year. Families of students are welcome from Nov 3-5 to come celebrate Ithaca's 125th anniversary! The weekend will be chock full of fun events for all ages.
